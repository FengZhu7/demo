Steven "Steve" Jobs was a creative genius who changed the world of computers and technology. He was born on February 24, 1955, to two graduate students, one who was a Syrian professor studying at the University of Wisconsin. Jobs was then adopted by an accountant and a machinist. Growing up in Silicon Valley and watching his father tinker on electronics, young Job was to the technology of the day. He finished high school, where he met his future Apple co-founder, Steven Wozniak. Jobs briefly attended Reed College, but didn’t like it.
